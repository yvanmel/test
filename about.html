About
Sorg og Samfund - or SOS - is a magazine for poetry and fashion.
The magazine was launched in Copenhagen in 2022 and has presented a wide range of new voices and established names from the fields of literature, fashion, visual art, academia, and activism.

Founding editors: Lars Skinnebach, Nynne Roberta Pedersen Pedersen, Ulrikke Bak.
Art Director: Lassi Bondesen.

SOS is a non-profit magazine and is financed by advertisements. Past issues have been supported by The Roskilde Festival Grant and The Danish Arts Foundation.
